How I work

I own the system.
Not the task list.

I've seen what happens when you ask one person to cover SEO, paid, content, brand, email, events, and social all at once. LinkedIn is full of those stories. Burnout, inconsistent output, and a company that ends up wondering why marketing “didn't work.”

That's not what I do. What I do is come in, diagnose where growth is actually leaking, build the right system to fix it, and make it run. I bring strategy and execution together, use AI to replace what should be automated, and hand off a machine that keeps working.

At WrmsKonnect, I was the Marketing Manager. I didn't try to do everything myself. I figured out where growth was leaking, decided what to prioritise, what to delegate, and what to kill. Paid acquisition, outbound, attribution — I built those systems, led the execution, and made sure every piece fed the next. The result: 2 to 326 customers and $8,150 MRR in seven months.

WrmsKonnect Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Aug 2025 — Mar 2026

“When bounce was 78%, I didn't A/B test the button. I ran a 94-person survey in 48 hours and found the real problem.”

Our CTR was strong at 4.1%. But the landing page bounce was 78%. The standard playbook says: test layout, test CTA, run variants. I ran scroll depth analysis, session recordings, and a 94-response user survey in 48 hours instead. The problem wasn't the page design at all. It was the hero copy triggering a fear objection that we'd never noticed. I rebuilt the section to address the objection directly. Bounce dropped from 78% to 42%. That's the difference between iterating on the wrong thing and diagnosing the right one.
